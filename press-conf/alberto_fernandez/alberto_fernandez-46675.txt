Wednesday, 22 January 2020
President Alberto Fernández arrived in Israel this morning. He was received at Tel Aviv international airport by Miri Regev, Israeli Minister of Culture and Sport; Yuval Rotem, Director General of the Israeli Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Modi Ephraim, Deputy Director General, Head of Latin American & Caribbean Division, at the Israeli Ministry of Foreign Affairs; and Gali Ronen, Israeli Ambassador to Argentina.
